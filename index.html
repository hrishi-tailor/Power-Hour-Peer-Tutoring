Why Do We Need This Website?

Many students face challenges when it comes to seeking help or learning proactively due to feelings of embarrassment or discomfort. This website provides a safe and anonymous platform, alleviating the hesitation often associated with visiting the Student Success Room in person.

Our goal is to empower students at Father Michael Goetz to identify their learning needs and benefit from peer-to-peer learning opportunities in a supportive and encouraging environment.
